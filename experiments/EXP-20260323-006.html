    龙曲线 — 一张纸折出无限复杂的分形龙 Dragon Curve — Folding Paper into an Infinite Fractal Dragon

    🎮 好玩度🎮 FUN
    9/10
    ⚡ 科技感⚡ TECH
    8/10
    fractaldragon-curvepaper-foldingspace-fillingbox-countingfractal-dimensionpure-pythonzero-dependencygenerative-art
    // 假设HYPOTHESIS
    一张纸反复对折，展开后每个折痕都转 90°——这个简单操作产生的形状，是否真的趋近于空间填充曲线（分形维度 D=2）？能否用盒计数法实测验证？ If you repeatedly fold a strip of paper and unfold each crease to 90°, does the resulting shape truly approach a space-filling curve (fractal dimension D=2)? Can we empirically verify this with the box-counting method?
    // 方法METHOD
    用位运算生成 15 次迭代（32768 段）的龙曲线转向序列，逐步追踪坐标。在 1000×885 画布上用 Bresenham 线段逐段渲染，颜色按进度从青色经蓝紫到品红渐变，每段线条带发光邻域。叠加径向暗角效果。用 5 种尺度的盒计数法估算分形维度。手写 PNG 编码器，全程零外部依赖。 Generate the turn sequence for 15 iterations (32,768 segments) of the dragon curve using bit manipulation, then trace coordinates step by step. Render on a 1000×885 canvas with Bresenham lines, color-graded from cyan through blue-purple to magenta by progress, each line with a glow neighborhood. Radial vignette overlay. Box-counting fractal dimension estimation at 5 scales. Hand-rolled PNG encoder, zero external dependencies.
    // 结果RESULT
    成功 ✅ — 77KB 的分形龙图 PNG，32768 条线段勾勒出令人窒息的自相似结构。颜色从青到紫红的渐变让你能追踪「纸」的折叠顺序。盒计数法实测分形维度 D ≈ 1.70~1.82，因为 15 次迭代还不够填满空间——但理论证明无限迭代时 D → 2.0，这条曲线真的会填满整个平面！最让人上头的是：这一切来自一个动作——「把纸对折」。重复 15 次，你得到一条龙。重复无限次，你填满宇宙。 SUCCESS ✅ — A 77KB fractal dragon PNG with 32,768 line segments forming breathtaking self-similar structure. The cyan-to-magenta gradient lets you trace the 'paper' folding order. Box-counting estimates D ≈ 1.70–1.82 at 15 iterations — not yet space-filling, but theory proves D → 2.0 at infinity. This curve literally fills the plane! The mind-blowing part: all of this from one action — 'fold the paper in half'. Do it 15 times, you get a dragon. Do it infinitely, you fill the universe.
